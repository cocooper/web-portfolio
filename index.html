It's Raining Chicken Balls!

Welcome to the home of the best place on the internet to learn about local Alberta chicken balls. We've got recipies. We've got cooking tips. We've got hot spots to check out. And best of all, we have the go-to knowledge about your favourite food on the planet!

Sooo Gooood!

What is a chicken ball you ask?... The chicken ball is a mouth-watering treat essential to any cultural dish. Packed with juicy chicken, an Alberta chicken ball can be coated with whatever you can dream up. Some of those ideas can be found on our recipies page so go check that out!

Cooking Tips

Fry 'em, deep fry 'em, bake 'em, do whatever you like... you can't go wrong. But if your are new at this, try one of these ideas. Smother your battered chicken balls with plum sauce and sesame seeds. You could pair your homemade Alberta chicken balls with a nice array of fried vegetables floating in teriyaki sauce.
