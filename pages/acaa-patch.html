ACAA Patch

Client: Alberta Central Air Ambulance

Role: Designer

Along with four others, Cole created an insignia patch for Alberta Central Air Ambulance. The pandemic forced this great company to work on overdrive for an extended period, and they wanted something bold to boost morale. Cole's main contributions were border layout, typography, and the mockup you see below. This project has reached its final stage, and the design is actively undergoing embroidery.
